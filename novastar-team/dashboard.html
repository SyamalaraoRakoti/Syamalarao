Pipeline Design Principle

The NovaStar pipeline utilizes a strict handoff protocol. Each agent operates as an independent service in a containerized pipeline, managed by the Program Manager's 'Quality Gate' protocol.

Governance & GDPR

Data Privacy Protocol

All PII (Personally Identifiable Information) must be anonymized (SHA-256) at the point of ingestion. No raw guest logs are persisted in agent-level application logs.
