The Journey

Our Restoration Process

01

Consultation & Assessment

Share your timepiece's story. Our specialists perform a thorough 47-point diagnostic to map the restoration path.

02

Disassembly & Cataloguing

Every component is photographed, documented, and catalogued before careful disassembly begins.

03

Restoration & Refinishing

Our master horologists restore each component using period-correct tools, materials, and techniques.

04

Assembly & Calibration

Precision reassembly followed by amplitude, beat error, and timing calibration to COSC standards.

05

Final Inspection & Delivery

A senior horologist personally verifies every detail before your timepiece is returned, ready for another generation.
