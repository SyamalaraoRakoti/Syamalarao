2. Top 5 Churn Predictors

  1. Profile completion <30% + no payment saved within 7 days of signup (Very Strong)
  2. Competing cafe within 400m + 5+ visits/month (Strong — 71% correlation)
  3. Monthly visits drop >50% vs 3-month average (Strong)
  4. No weekend visit for 3+ consecutive weekends (Moderate — Weekend Explorers)
  5. No visit in November after visiting in October (Moderate — Afternoon Treaters, seasonal)

3. Store-Level Variance

Churn is NOT uniform. Business district stores (8) show 4% MoM decline (72% Commuter concentration). Suburban retail parks (3) have 78% dormant rate. University stores (5) have seasonal churn offset by new signups. Mixed residential/commercial stores (12) are most resilient with 2% churn.

Agent 2: Designer
Personalised Re-engagement Strategy Blueprint

1. Segment-Specific Re-engagement Journeys

Commuters

Step 1: 14 weekdays without visit → Monday 7:30am push
Step 2: "EUR 2 off your regular latte before 9am at [PRIMARY STORE]"
Step 3: 5-visit streak challenge → free pastry on completion

Weekend Explorers

Step 1: 3 missed weekends → Friday 5pm push
Step 2: "EUR 2 off oat-milk flat white + pastry, any location this weekend"
Step 3: Post-redemption poll → favourite location → recurring Friday push

Afternoon Treaters

Step 1: First Tuesday monthly (Oct-Mar) → 1:30pm email [EMAIL-ONLY]
Step 2: "20% off hot drink + pastry combo, Tue-Thu 2-4pm at [RESIDENTIAL STORE]"
Step 3: "Afternoon Club" — visit 2x/month, get 3rd seasonal special free

Once-Only Signups

Designer decision: DO NOT RE-ENGAGE. Budget redirected to onboarding fix — mandatory 3-field profile completion at sign-up. Profiles incomplete after 7 days are auto-archived.

Former Regulars

Step 1: Within 7 days of competitor opening → immediate push
Step 2: "Your first drink is on us — any drink, any size"
Step 3: Welcome-back sequence: updated preferences + 10% loyalty bonus for 4 visits
