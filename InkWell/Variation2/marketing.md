# Launch Campaign Copy: Inkwell Rewritten
**Author:** Cathal (Lifecycle + BookTok Copywriter)

## 1. Launch Email
**Subject:** A new chapter for Inkwell, and a card for you.

**Preview Text:** We've missed you. Let's start a new chapter together.

**Body:**
Dear reader,

We know that life moves fast, and sometimes the quietest corners—the ones with the creaking floors and the best recommendations—get left behind in the rush of the everyday.

Footfall at Inkwell has changed, and we’ve heard you: our old stamp card wasn’t working for the way you shop now. But we haven't stopped reading, and we haven't stopped searching for the next great story.

We’re launching the **Inkwell Library Card**. It’s not a piece of card you’ll lose in a drawer; it’s a digital *ex-libris* bookplate that lives on your phone. It unlocks free books, members-only author events, and late-night quiet hours.

Claim yours here, and let’s rewrite what a bookstore can be.

[CTA: Claim your Library Card]

*Inkwell Books. 1991–2026.*

---
*You are receiving this because you’ve shared your details with us. Inkwell Books values your privacy. We will only use your email to share Inkwell news and events. You can unsubscribe at any time.*

The shop you remember, updated for the way you read now. 🕯️✨

### B. TikTok/Reels (Script: Fast-paced, warm, literary aesthetic)
**Visual:** Quick cuts of a book being opened, a hand taking a book from a shelf, then a close-up of the glowing digital Library Card on a phone screen.
**Audio:** "Remember Inkwell? We’ve missed you. We’re changing how we do things—no more lost stamp cards. Meet your new Library Card. Digital, literary, and ready to get you back into the shop for author nights and free reads. Link in bio. Come say hi."
